Crystal collector Game
Let's see what you got!.


Instructions

You should see four crystals, a random number to which you will match, and your point box. In order to win the game, you must click the crystals in a way that causes your total points to match the random number given to you at the start of each round. The values behind each crystal are different from one another, and can only be discovered through clicking each crystal (they will, however, all have points ranging from one to twelve, only). After each round the values behind each crystal change, as well as the random number you are trying to match. If your total points does not EXACTLY match the random number, you lose...
